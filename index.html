Projects

PowerGymGirls

On this project I created the brand "PowerGymGirls" to fit in the fitness industry, my aim was to present a solution designed to elevate the gym experience for both staff and clients alike. At the core of my project lies the Canvas app, meticulously crafted for both mobile and tablet platforms. This intuitive application serves as the nerve center of gym management, offering seamless navigation and robust functionality for our staff. The goal was to ensure efficiency and productivity on the gym. In this project we embedded the power of Power BI, offering deep insights into every facet of our gym. From user demographics to modality preferences and competitor analysis, this tool was used to make informed decisions that drive growth and innovation. And for the backbone of the business – our clients – I developed a Model Driven app, providing a centralized platform for managing client information and registrations. With streamlined workflows and tailored functionalities, it was ensured that every interaction with the clients is personalized and seamless. Together, these components form the foundation of the gym brand, reflecting the unwavering commitment to excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction.
